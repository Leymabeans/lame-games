January News

The last few weeks here at Lame Games it has been very busy, and we are excited to share some updates with you!

We have finally made the switch and the Lame Games website is no longer built with PHP, and instead with more updated technologies. We are currently switching our hosting provider so the temporary website domain is hosted with our friends at Netlify at lamegames.netlify.com .

We are excited to get Lame Games up and running with our application sometime in the near future! We have been working hard on connecting the application to our server and database, so we will keep our fingers crossed that will get it all configured soon.

With the release of our application, 1.0 versions of our first two games will be released for everyone in the Lame Games community to enjoy. Names of the games as well as all the details will be released about a week before the launching of our application.

Thanks for reading,

-Lame Games
